## Coverage Gaps

This server does **not** include:

- Arabic-only SFDA publications (we only index English-language materials)

- SFDA enforcement actions, penalty decisions, or Warning Letters
- Court decisions interpreting SFDA regulations
- Drug, biologic, and cosmetic regulations outside the medical device scope
- Draft regulations or consultation papers unless finalised

- Device-specific registration conditions or post-approval commitments issued to individual companies
- Laboratory accreditation requirements under SASO

## Recommended Verification Workflow

Before relying on any regulatory information retrieved through this server:

1. **Identify the relevant SFDA regulation or guidance** using this server's search tools
2. **Retrieve the official document** directly from the SFDA portal at https://sfda.gov.sa/en/regulations
3. **Confirm the version and effective date** — check whether the document has been revised or superseded
4. **Read the full original text** — do not rely solely on summaries or excerpts
5. **Consult qualified regulatory affairs counsel** for interpretation and application to your specific device
6. **Check for related guidance** — SFDA may have issued supplementary documents on the same topic
